Chikamaglur - Travel Story

  • Chikmagalur is a beautiful district situated on the western part of Karnataka.It is mostly popular for its coffee plantations and a pleasant climate
  • The literal meaning of Chikmagalur is ‘Land of the younger daughter.’
  • There is a rich history attached to how Chikmagalur got its name. It is said that years ago the land of this district was given as a dowry to the younger daughter of a legendary chief who ruled Sakrepatna- Rukmangada. And that is how it was named.

Must Places to visit in Chikmagalur

  1. Kudremukh National Park

  2. Mullayanagiri Hills

  3. Hebbe Falls

  4. Kallathigiri Falls
